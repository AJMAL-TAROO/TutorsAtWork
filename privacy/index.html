Privacy Policy

How TutorsAtWork handles information used to provide its learning services.

Effective: 30 June 2026

Information we handle

We may handle account and contact details, login credentials, classroom information, attendance, feedback, comments, and files such as notes or homework. Basic technical information may also be processed when you use the service.

How we use information

We use this information to provide and secure TutorsAtWork, authenticate users, manage classrooms, support learning activities, communicate with users, and provide technical support. We do not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising.

Storage and sharing

Information may be stored and processed using service providers such as Google Firebase. It may be visible to authorised tutors, students, and administrators where required for the service. We may also disclose information when required by law.

Retention and security

We retain information while it is needed to provide the service, manage an account, or meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, although no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, or delete your personal information, subject to applicable legal and educational record requirements. A parent or guardian may contact us about a student's information.

Changes

We may update this policy when the service or legal requirements change. The latest version will remain available on this page.
